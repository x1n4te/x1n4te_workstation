## The 8 Papers You Must Read

| # | Paper | Year | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Attention Is All You Need (Vaswani et al.) | 2017 | The transformer. Everything builds on this. |
| 2 | BERT (Devlin et al.) | 2018 | Bidirectional pretraining, NLP paradigm shift |
| 3 | GPT-2: Language Models are Unsupervised Multitask Learners (Radford et al.) | 2019 | Zero-shot, scaling language models |

## Reading Strategy

- Don't read papers front-to-back. Read abstract, then the architecture diagram, then the results.
- For each paper, code the key idea (even in pseudocode).
- Focus on HOW the paper changed the field, not every experimental detail.
