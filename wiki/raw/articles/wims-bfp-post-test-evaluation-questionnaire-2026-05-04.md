## Researcher Analysis Guide

### Suggested Analysis

Compute Weighted Mean per category, interpreted using:

| Mean Range | Interpretation |
|------------|---------------|
| 4.21 – 5.00 | Very Satisfactory |
| 3.41 – 4.20 | Satisfactory |
| 2.61 – 3.40 | Neutral |
| 1.81 – 2.60 | Unsatisfactory |
| 1.00 – 1.80 | Poor |

### A. Questionnaire Validation

Show that the instrument was reviewed before use. Include:
- Adviser review
- Expert validation
- Face validity
- Content relevance check

**Suggested Statement:**
> The questionnaire was reviewed by subject experts and the research adviser to ensure clarity, relevance, and alignment with the study objectives.

### B. Reliability Test of Questionnaire (Recommended)

Compute Cronbach's Alpha:

| Alpha Value | Interpretation |
|------------|---------------|
| ≥ 0.90 | Excellent |
| 0.80 – 0.89 | Good |
| 0.70 – 0.79 | Acceptable |

**Suggested Statement:**
> A pilot test may be conducted to assess internal consistency reliability using Cronbach's Alpha.

### C. Sampling Justification

State: number of respondents, why selected, relevant expertise.

**Example:**
> Respondents were selected using purposive sampling based on their technical knowledge, operational relevance, or familiarity with similar systems.

### D. Ethical Compliance

Include: consent form, voluntary participation, confidentiality, right to withdraw.
