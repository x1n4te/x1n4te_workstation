## Core Thesis

LLMs represent a fundamentally new computing paradigm — Software 3.0. Unlike Software 1.0 (writing explicit code) or Software 2.0 (training neural networks with datasets), Software 3.0 programs through natural language prompting, with the context window serving as the lever over an LLM interpreter that performs computation in the digital information space.

---

## Key Quotes

- "What is the piece of text to copy-paste to your agent? That's the programming paradigm."
- "Menu Genen shouldn't exist. It's working in the old paradigm. The app shouldn't exist."
- "I don't want to do anything. What is the thing I should copy-paste to my agent?"
- "You can outsource your thinking but you can't outsource your understanding."
