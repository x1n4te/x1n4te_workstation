# Quick Start Guide

This guide will get you up and running with the Smart Parenting App in just a few minutes.

---

## 1. Create Your Account

When you first open the app:

1. Tap **Sign Up** on the welcome screen.
2. Enter your **full name**, **email address**, and a **password** (minimum 6 characters).
3. Tap **Create Account**.
4. Check your email for a confirmation link and tap it to verify your account.
5. Return to the app and **Log In**.

> **Tip:** Use a password you can remember — the app won't log you out automatically.

---

## 2. Add Your First Child

After logging in, you'll see the Dashboard. If you haven't added a child yet:

1. Tap the **+ Add** button in the child selector at the top.
2. Enter your child's **name** and **date of birth**.
3. (Optional) Set a **gender** — this helps the BMI calculator provide accurate assessments.
4. Tap **Next** through the wizard steps:
   - **Routine** — Set typical bedtimes, wake-up times, and meal times.
   - **BMI** — Enter height and weight if you know them (ages 2–5 only).
   - **Avatar** — Pick an emoji or upload a photo.
5. Tap **Save**.

Your child's profile is now created.

---

## 3. Log an Activity

To record something your child just did:

1. Tap the **Activities** tab at the bottom.
2. Make sure you're in **Log** mode (not Schedule).
3. Tap an activity type:
   - **Screen Time** — TV, tablet, phone
   - **Sleep** — Nighttime sleep
   - **Nap** — Daytime sleep
   - **Meals** — Breakfast, lunch, snack, dinner
   - **Physical Activity** — Swimming, running, playing outside
   - **Learning** — Reading, homework, educational apps
4. Enter the time range or duration.
5. Add any details (device type, food groups, quality rating).
6. Tap **Log Activity**.

A green checkmark confirms the log was saved.

---

## 4. View Your History

1. Tap the **History** tab.
2. Use the calendar to pick a day.
3. See all logged activities for that day.
4. Tap **Stats** at the bottom to see weekly or monthly charts.

---

## 5. Get AI Insights

Once you have a few days of data:

1. Tap the **AI Insights** tab.
2. Tap **Generate Insights**.
3. The app analyzes the last 28 days of activity and provides personalized recommendations.
4. Recommendations are grouped by category (Sleep, Meals, etc.) with badges showing which are the most recent.

> **Note:** Insights are generated at most once per day per child to save resources.

---

## 6. Set Up Notifications (Optional)

By default, no notifications are enabled. To turn them on:

1. Tap the **Settings** tab.
2. Tap your child's card.
3. Scroll to **Notifications**.
4. Toggle ON the reminders you want (bedtime, meals, etc.).
5. Tap **Save**.

The app will now remind you according to the routine times you set during setup.
