# Settings

The Settings tab is your central hub for managing your account, your children's profiles, and app preferences.

---

## Your Profile

At the top, you'll see:
- Your avatar (or initials)
- Your name
- Your email address

**Tap "Edit Profile"** to change your name or upload a new avatar photo.

---

## All Notifications Toggle

A master switch at the top of Settings controls notifications globally:
- **ON:** Schedules reminders for all children, respecting each child's individual preferences
- **OFF:** Cancels all reminders immediately

> This does **not** override per-child settings. When you turn it back on, each child gets only the reminders they individually have enabled.

---

## Your Children

Each child appears as a card showing:
- Avatar or initials
- Name and age
- A colored background (rotates through a palette)

**Tap a child's card** to open their detailed settings.

**Tap "+ Add Child"** to create a new profile via the wizard.

If you have no children, you'll see a dashed empty-state banner prompting you to add one.

---

## Account Actions

- **Change Email** — Update your login email (requires current password for security)
- **Change Password** — Update your password (requires current password)
- **Privacy Policy** — Read the app's data privacy policy
- **Help & FAQ** — Browse frequently asked questions with research citations

---

## Sign Out

Tap **Sign Out** at the bottom. A confirmation modal appears, followed by a brief "Goodbye" animation before returning you to the login screen.

---

## Child Settings (Per-Child)

Tap any child's card to manage their individual configuration.

### Profile
- **Name** — Edit the child's display name
- **Date of Birth** — Change birthdate (affects age calculations and BMI)
- **Avatar** — Pick an emoji or upload a photo

### Body Metrics
- **Gender** — Male or Female (affects WHO BMI assessment)
- **Height & Weight** — Entered in metric (cm and kg)

If your child is between **2 and 5 years old**, a live **BMI preview** appears as you type, showing:
- Z-score and percentile
- WHO category (underweight, normal, overweight, obese)

> BMI is also auto-computed by the database whenever you save.

### Routine

Set daily routine times using quick-pick chips or manual input:
- Bedtime
- Wake-up time
- Breakfast, Lunch, Snack, Dinner
- Nap time
- Activity time
- Learning time

Each time field uses hour/minute steppers with an AM/PM toggle. Tap a quick-pick chip to auto-fill common times (e.g., "8:00 PM" for bedtime).

### Notifications

Ten individually toggleable reminder types:
- 🌙 Bedtime Reminder (30 min before)
- ☀️ Wake-Up Reminder
- 🍳 Breakfast Reminder
- 🍚 Lunch Reminder
- 🍎 Snack Reminder
- 🍲 Dinner Reminder
- 😴 Nap Reminder
- 🏊 Activity Reminder
- 📚 Learning Reminder
- 📊 Weekly Growth Check (every Monday at 9 AM)

**Default for new children:** All OFF. You must explicitly enable the ones you want.

### Delete Child

At the bottom of the screen, **"Delete Child Profile"** allows you to remove a child:
- A confirmation modal warns: *"All activity records will remain, but the profile and reminders will be removed."*
- Activities are preserved for data integrity — only the profile is deleted

---

## Pull to Refresh

Swipe down on the main Settings screen to refresh your children list from the server.
