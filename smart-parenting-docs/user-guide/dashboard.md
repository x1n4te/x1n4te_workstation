# Dashboard

The Dashboard is the first thing you see when you open the app. It gives you a snapshot of your child's day — what they've done, what's coming up, and quick actions to log new activities.

---

## Greeting & Date

At the top, you'll see a friendly greeting based on the time of day:
- **"Good morning"** before noon
- **"Good afternoon"** from noon to 6 PM
- **"Good evening"** after 6 PM

Your first name appears below the greeting, along with today's date (e.g., "Saturday, April 25").

---

## Child Selector

A horizontal row of chips shows all your children's profiles. Each chip displays:
- An avatar initial or photo
- The child's name
- Their age in years

**To switch children:** Just tap a different chip. The entire dashboard updates instantly to show that child's data.

**To add a child:** Tap the **+ Add** chip at the end of the row, or the dashed "Add your first child" banner if you haven't added any yet.

---

## Quick Log

A prominent coral button labeled **"Log an Activity"** sits below the child selector. Tap it to jump straight to the Activities tab to record something.

---

## Today's Stats

A 2×2 grid of cards summarizes today's logged data:

| Card | What It Shows | Color |
|------|---------------|-------|
| **Screen Time** | Total minutes today (e.g., "1h 30m") | Coral |
| **Sleep** | Total sleep logged today (e.g., "9h 0m") | Green |
| **Meals** | Meals tracked out of 3 (e.g., "2/3") | Amber |
| **Education** | Total learning minutes today (e.g., "45m") | Purple |

These update in real time as you log activities throughout the day.

---

## Upcoming Activities

This section shows scheduled activities for today and future days:
- Activity type icon and color badge
- Date label ("Today" or "Sat, Apr 25")
- Time range (e.g., "7:00 AM → 8:00 AM")
- Status badge:
  - **⏳ Upcoming** — hasn't started yet
  - **Log** button appears when the activity's start time has passed

**Actions on each item:**
- **Log** — Convert a scheduled activity into a completed log
- **Update** — Change the time or duration
- **Cancel** — Delete the schedule permanently (with confirmation)

If nothing is scheduled, you'll see "No upcoming activities" with a prompt to schedule one.

---

## Recent Activity

A list of today's logged activities, newest first:
- Activity icon with colored dot
- Human-readable description (e.g., "Sleep — 9h 0m (good)")
- Time of day

Maximum 5 shown. Tap "+N more activities" to see the rest in the History tab.

---

## AI Insights Banner

At the very bottom, a card invites you to check AI-generated recommendations:
- "Get personalized recommendations for [child's name]"
- Tap to go to the AI Insights tab

---

## Pull to Refresh

Swipe down anywhere on the dashboard to reload all data from the server. This is useful if you logged an activity on another device or want the latest schedules.

---

## Empty States

- **No children added:** Dashed banner prompting you to add your first child.
- **No activities today:** "No activities yet — tap Log to record one."
- **No upcoming schedules:** "No upcoming activities — tap here to schedule."
