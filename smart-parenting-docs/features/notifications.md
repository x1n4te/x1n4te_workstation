# Notifications

The Smart Parenting App can remind you about your child's daily routine — bedtime, meals, naps, and more. Notifications are **opt-in** by default: nothing is enabled until you turn it on.

---

## Types of Notifications

There are **10 reminder types**, each tied to a routine time you set in your child's profile:

| # | Reminder | When It Fires |
|---|----------|---------------|
| 1 | Bedtime Reminder | 30 minutes before bedtime |
| 2 | Wake-Up Reminder | At wake-up time |
| 3 | Breakfast Reminder | At breakfast time |
| 4 | Lunch Reminder | At lunch time |
| 5 | Snack Reminder | At snack time |
| 6 | Dinner Reminder | At dinner time |
| 7 | Nap Reminder | At nap time |
| 8 | Activity Reminder | At physical activity time |
| 9 | Learning Reminder | At learning time |
| 10 | Weekly Growth Check | Every Monday at 9:00 AM |

---

## Default Behavior

**For new children:** All 10 notifications are **OFF**.

When you create a child through the wizard, the app explicitly saves every notification type as disabled. No reminders are scheduled until you choose to enable them.

**For existing children:** Their saved preferences are never changed automatically.

---

## How to Enable Notifications

1. Go to the **Settings** tab.
2. Tap your child's card.
3. Scroll to the **Notifications** section.
4. Toggle ON the reminders you want.
5. Tap **Save** at the top.

The app immediately schedules the enabled reminders based on the routine times in your child's profile.

> If a routine time is not set (e.g., no bedtime entered), the corresponding reminder cannot fire even if the toggle is ON.

---

## Master Toggle

At the top of the Settings screen, the **All Notifications** switch controls everything globally:

- **Turning OFF:** Cancels all reminders for all children immediately.
- **Turning ON:** Reschedules reminders for all children, but only the ones each child individually has enabled.

**Example:**
- Child A has 3 reminders ON → Those 3 reschedule
- Child B has all reminders OFF → Nothing happens
- Child C has all 10 ON → All 10 reschedule

---

## Scheduled Activity Reminders

Separately from routine reminders, the app also sends notifications for individual scheduled activities:
- A reminder when the activity starts
- A reminder when the scheduled window ends

These are **not affected** by the notification toggles above. They are always scheduled for pending activities.

---

## Troubleshooting

**I'm not receiving reminders even though they're turned on:**
- Make sure the corresponding routine time is set in your child's profile.
- Check that your phone's system settings allow notifications from the Smart Parenting app.
- Verify the master **All Notifications** toggle is ON.

**I get too many reminders:**
- Turn OFF specific reminder types in your child's notification settings.
- Alternatively, turn OFF the master toggle to silence everything.

**Reminders are firing at the wrong time:**
- Check your child's routine times in Settings → Child → Routine.
- Ensure your phone's timezone is set correctly.
